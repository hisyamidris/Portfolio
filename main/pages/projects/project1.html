UAV LiDAR Project
Project description Our product is a quadcopter with the capability to capture and save images of its environment 5 meters or less in a 240 degree sweep in front of it. This allows the user to capture clear imaging to build a map of an unknown environment. Note the final product will provide quality imaging data that could be stitched together for a complete environmental map.
